01 — Our Story

Not just a 3D printer company. The beginning of advanced manufacturing in Iraq.

Why we exist

Why MATIC Was Founded

We saw a generation of engineers, designers and creators held back by access — not ability. MATIC was founded to put world-class digital manufacturing directly into Iraqi hands.

The vision

The Manufacturing Vision

We design and manufacture fast, reliable, easy-to-use 3D printers — engineered locally, supported locally, and built to a global standard of quality.

The future

The Future Of Local Innovation

Every printer we ship is a workshop, a classroom and a factory in one. We're building the tools that let Iraq prototype, produce and create without limits.

The impact

Impact On Iraq's Technology Ecosystem

From universities to startups to established factories, MATIC seeds an ecosystem where ideas become objects — accelerating an entire nation's capacity to build.
